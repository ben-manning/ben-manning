## About

With over 10 years of experience in web development, I've learned how to build websites that are both visually appealing and highly functional. I thrive on crafting digital experiences that make an impact, from streamlined user interfaces to responsive designs that work seamlessly on any device.

I also bring extensive instructional experience, having spent years mentoring developers and teaching web technologies. Whether leading workshops or guiding students one-on-one, I excel at breaking down complex topics and making learning engaging and accessible.

Outside of work, I enjoy playing drums, listening to music, and bowling. Most of all, I cherish time spent with my partner and our two dogs, Lucy and Harvey, whether we're out exploring or just relaxing at home.
